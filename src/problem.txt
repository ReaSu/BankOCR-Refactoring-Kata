OCR Kata Requirements

1. Accepts four strings of characters ("lines") and returns a
   nine-digit account number as a string. Like this:
        " _   _   _   _   _   _   _   _   _  "
        "|_| |_| |_| |_| |_| |_| |_| |_| |_| "
        "|_| |_| |_| |_| |_| |_| |_| |_| |_| "
        "                                    "
   Returns: "888888888"

2. Returns ? for an invalid character.

3. Returns ILL when it contains invalid character. "888888?88 ILL"
